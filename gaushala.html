Gaushala

About Gaushala

Mission of our trust is Gau seva and digital hospital to provide free of cost medical treatment to helpless and wounded Gau Mata. In this regards and understanding the pain of Gau Mata. Under the Chairmanship of shri indradevji maharaj “radha rasbihari bahuuddeshiya sanstha” has initiated in this direction and purchased 10 acres land on National Highway 6 near sawkheda hol Jalgoan to construct a Cow hospital. Ashram believes that taking the responsibilities of Gau mata is our duty. Ashram takes care of more than 300 Gau Matas, feeding them with proper nutritious meals Fodders, Turi, Khal, Binola, Choker, Gur etc. Needful medication and proper treatment. Most of the cows in ashram gua shala are the ones found on sides of roads wounded, met with an accident ,guruji gets all such cows give them proper medication and if any farmer wants cow ashram provides them for free.Theres big open plot for cows where they are taken twice a day and left for graving apart from their big shed .Also we have separate shed for calfs. Pregnant cows are taken care very well with regular check up by veterian doctors. Ashram only takes required amount of milk from cow rest is kept for their calf .
