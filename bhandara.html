About Bhandara

Ashram runs the Non profit Organizational charity of Annadan which serves hundreds of people with time to time meals. Annadan runs on daily basis, where everyone gets proper and healthy meals. Everyday more than 700 people have bhojan Prasad in ashram which is prepared in ashram bhojanalay .All the measures of hygiene are taken to prepare Prasad bhojan as it is strictly vaishnav. Everyday lunch and dinner consist of DAL,RICE,1 VEGETABLE ,ROTI AND LASSI most of times even sweet dish in included. With proper care of diabetic patients from vriddha ashram.Also morning breakfast /tea and evening tea and snacks are served in annadan to everyone at no cost. Vegetables prepared in bhojan Prasad are from ashrams organic farm. We wish to have a bigger space for kitchen with latest and big cooking equipments, as our bhakt family is increasing every day.

Many people come in ashram to celebrate their parents, kids or their own birthdays or anniversary by doing annadan among vriddh ashram mata pita or vedic school kids or in gau shala in a proper Indian way which of course makes it most memorable for them.
